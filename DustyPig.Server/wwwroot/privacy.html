Dusty Pig collects the following information:
  • Account credientials (encrypted)
  • Playback progress


This information is used soley for functionality. Specifically:
  • Limit users to their own media
  • Playback progress - to enable 'Resume Watching' and 'Up Next'


Dusty Pig will never share or sell any of this information to anyone. It is solely for the functionality of the platform.
